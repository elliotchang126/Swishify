Welcome to Swishify!

Swishify lets users look at the 2022-23 All-NBA players shot data and their career profile. The main two functions of Swishify is to look at a player's profile and their shot chart for the 2022-23 regular season.

On the left side is the player profile. By default, Swishify will load the page with LeBron James pre-selected. Just click on the dropdown below the chart to change the profile to your desired player. This will list their general information, as well as career average statistics.

On the right, use the buttons to select which data you want to look. Click on the dropdown to select the player whose data you wish to see. The shot chart will generate a chart displaying where the player shot from, with green circles as makes and red circles as misses. The zone chart will display the player's field goal percentage by zone compared to the league average. The comparison chart compares the two players selected, showing their field goal percentages by zone. The second player dropdown is only available for the comparison chart.
